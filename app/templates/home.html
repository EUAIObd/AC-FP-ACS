CryptoSuite

Applied Cryptography Project

Encrypt, decrypt, and hash your messages and files using modern cryptographic algorithms.

Services.

CryptoSuite offers secure, easy-to-use cryptographic tools for everyone. Our services include:

  • Symmetric Encryption: Encrypt and decrypt messages or files using AES, DES, and 3DES algorithms.
  • Asymmetric Encryption: Secure your data with RSA and ECC public-key cryptography.
  • Hashing: Generate cryptographic hashes (SHA-256, SHA-512, MD5, BLAKE2) for text and files.
  • Digital Signatures: Sign and verify messages using ECC for authenticity and integrity.

All operations are performed locally in your browser or on your device for maximum privacy and security.

About CryptoSuite

CryptoSuite is an educational project for demonstrating modern applied cryptography. It is designed for students, professionals, and anyone interested in learning or using cryptographic algorithms in a user-friendly way.

The project is open source and welcomes contributions from the community.
